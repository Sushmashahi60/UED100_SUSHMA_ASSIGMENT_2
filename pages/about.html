Our Story

CommBank has been serving our local community for over 35 years, providing reliable banking services and building lasting relationships with our customers. We've evolved from a traditional community bank to a modern financial institution that embraces technology while maintaining our personal touch.

Founded in 1985, we started with just 5 employees and a vision to serve our community better.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional banking services while supporting the growth and prosperity of our community through personalized financial solutions, innovative technology, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.
