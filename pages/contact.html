Frequently Asked Questions

What are your customer service hours?

Our customer service is available Monday-Friday 8AM-8PM, and Saturday 9AM-5PM. Emergency support is available 24/7 for account security issues.

How can I open a new account?

You can open an account online, over the phone, or by visiting any of our branch locations. You'll need a valid ID and proof of address to get started.

What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?

Contact us immediately at 1-800-BANK-STYLE or through our mobile app to report and block your card. We'll issue a replacement card within 2-3 business days.

How do I reset my online banking password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on our login page, or contact customer service. For security, password resets require identity verification.
